Group1 - opinion

Simon’s Structure

Introduction

(background)

I strongly hold that (our response).

Paragraph 1

On the one hand, I believe that ...

Firstly,secondly,finally...

To be more specific, (fact 1-2);

Not to mention that, (fact 1-3).

Exemplification

Furthermore, the fact that (point 2 mid level) indicates that (our response).

Take the case of (character), who / which (process). As a result, (result).

Had it not been for sth.(process paraphrase), (character) would never (result paraphrase).

Paragraph 2

However,...

The main reason is that...

Another key benefit of ... is ...

A final point is that ...

Concession

Nevertheless, a voice arises that (opposite response and point in grand opening).

Ironically, (facts against opposite detail).

Therefore, (point 3).

Conclusion

In conclusion, I disagree with the view that...

Structure

  1. introduction
  2. causes
  3. solutions
  4. conclusion: summarize the problem and steps

solutions

Vince's Structure

Key elements

  1. 5 paragraphs
  2. 350+ words

Structure

Argument

  • The Beginning
  • Point 1 + Reasoning
  • Point 2 + Exemplification
  • Point 3 + Concession
  • The End
tips
  • point尽量偏向宏观，便于拓展
  • 论点之间的逻辑关系必须紧密流畅

5 paragraphs

1. The Beginning

template

It is hard to deny that (opposite detail). Such a fact leads impressionable people to generate the opinion that (opposite response).

However, such a statement suffers from both logical and factual fallacies, and it should be examined meticulously.

As far as (Point 1), (Point 2) and (Point 3) are concerned, I strongly hold that (our response).

key phrases
  • appears adj.
  • simply due to...

2. Reasoning

three stages

层层递进，宏观\(\rightarrow\)微观

template

First and foremost, (point 1), as (fact 1-1).

For instance / To illustrate / To be more specific, (fact 1-2);

In addition / Not to mention that / Furthermore, (fact 1-3).

(Optional filler: 字数不足补充：summary/conclusion/what if/a small example)

tip
  • 所有fact必须服务于point 1，不能跑题

3. Exemplification

template

Furthermore, the fact that (point 2 mid level) indicates that (our response).

Take the case of (character), who / which (process). As a result, (result).

Had it bot been for (process paraphrase), (character) would never (result paraphrase).

Example

Concession

template

Nevertheless, a voice arises that (opposite response and point in grand opening).

Ironically, (facts against opposite detail).

Therefore, (point 3).

Ending

template

In a nutshell, I maintain that (response paraphrase of point 1,2,3).

Admittedly, as my favorite quote from Leibniz goes, there are no two identical leaves in the world. Everyone has their own unique perspective and some people may oppose me.

However, I believe they will compromise after being exposed to my article.

tips
  • points的论述禅师必须详细且通顺
